Sustainable energy is energy that meets the needs of the present generations without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It is about finding clean, renewable sources of energy that replenish themselves and do not harm the environment.

Key sources of sustainable energy include:
1. Solar Energy: Harnessing light and heat from the sun.
2. Wind Energy: Using wind turbines to generate electricity.
3. Hydroelectric Power: Generating energy from flowing water.
4. Geothermal Energy: Using heat from within the Earth.
5. Biomass: Organic matter used as a fuel.

Transitioning to sustainable energy is critical for mitigating climate change, reducing pollution, and ensuring energy security.
